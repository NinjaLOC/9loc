9loc's Bio

9loc aka Cloud9 of NinjaLOC™ is a Recording Artist, Producer, Studio Engineer, Promoter, Designer & founder of NinjaLOC & the 7 Artist super group known as “☥ßlaçk Magiçk Syndiçate☥”.
